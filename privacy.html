Who we are

High Street Digital provides website, SEO, automation, and digital support services for local businesses.

Information we collect

We may collect your name, business name, email address, phone number, service interest, and message when you contact us or request a review.

How we use information

We use your information to respond to enquiries, provide quotes, deliver services, manage client relationships, improve our website, and meet legal obligations.

Sharing information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with trusted service providers where needed to operate our website, manage communication, or deliver client work.

Our website may use essential cookies and privacy-conscious analytics to understand website performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data retention

We keep enquiry information only as long as needed for the purpose it was collected, unless legal or accounting requirements require longer retention.

Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your personal information by emailing enquiries@highstreetdigital.uk.

Contact
